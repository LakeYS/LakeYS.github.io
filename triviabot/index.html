An open-source trivia bot for Discord, powered by OpenTDB.

TriviaBot brings multiplayer trivia games to your Discord server! With over 2,500 questions and 24 categories, TriviaBot offers endless trivia fun.

Categories include Film, Television, Anime & Manga, Sports, Science & nature, and more.

To use the bot, simply add it to your server and type trivia help in a text channel. TriviaBot is still a work-in-progress, so there are plenty of new features on the way!

This bot is powered by discord.js and the Open Trivia Database (OpenTDB).
